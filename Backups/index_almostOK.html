Vessel Registration Services

Registering your vessel doesn't have to be complicated! We ensure that your vessel is properly registered and compliant with maritime regulations, quickly, legally, and without complications, meeting all necessary documentation and requirements.

Our services include:

  • 🔹 New Vessel Registration for Private yachts, Commercial ships, Fishing vessels, Offshore support vessels, Pleasure crafts
  • 🔹 Change of Ownership
  • 🔹 Flag Registration
  • 🔹 Vessel Documentation & Compliance
  • 🔹 Registration Renewal
  • 🔹 Mortgage Registration
  • 🔹 Crew Endorsements
  • 🔹 RO Services ensuring compliance with SOLAS, MARPOL, ISM Code, ISPS Code

Offshore Corporate Services

We provide comprehensive offshore corporate support, including:

  • 🔹 Establishment of New Entities in multiple offshore and international jurisdictions (Seychelles, Bahamas, Marshall Islands, Panama, Belize, Nevis, Delaware, BVI, Cayman Islands)
  • 🔹 Corporate Structuring & Re-structuring tailored to business needs
  • 🔹 Constitutional Changes (amendments to Articles/Memorandum, name changes, share changes, director changes, mergers, redomiciliations, dissolutions, etc.)

  • 🔹 Due Diligence / KYC / AML Compliance Assistance
  • 🔹 Corporate Bank Account Opening Support with international banking institutions
  • 🔹 Provision of Registered Office & Corporate Administration Services
  • 🔹 Annual Maintenance & Compliance Support
  • 🔹 Corporate Document Preparation & Filing
  • 🔹 Liaison with Registries, Authorities, and Financial Institutions
